Hello!

Steps:

  • Open the gallery and load an image
  • Stare at the image for the specified time
  • Open the front facing camera and take a picture of your toungue

    • The app analyzes the color content of the selfie to estimate how much you love the person in the image.

    The premise here is that when you stare at something that raises an emotional response, your body reflects it.
    The brain releases oxytocin, heart rate and blod flow subtly increase resulting in a change in tissue color.

    Please note that because android cameras come in every shape and size, this app cannot guarantee 100% accuracy!
